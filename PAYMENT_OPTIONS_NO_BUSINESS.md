## ✅ Better Alternatives

**Stripe doesn't require a "business account"** - just a regular account:

1. **Go to**: https://stripe.com
2. **Sign up** with your email (difaziotennis@gmail.com)
3. **Add** your bank account
4. **Get API keys** (free, instant)
5. **Add to Vercel** environment variables

**Benefits:**
- ✅ No business account needed
- ✅ Accept credit/debit cards
- ✅ Same fees as PayPal (2.9% + $0.30)
- ✅ Better for online businesses
- ✅ Easy setup

**Setup time**: ~20 minutes
